Post-Adoption Support

Adopting a pet is a wonderful experience, but it requires ongoing care and attention. Here are some helpful tips to ensure a smooth transition for your new furry friend:

  • Establish a consistent routine for feeding, playtime, and walks.
  • Schedule regular vet visits for vaccinations and health check-ups.
  • Use positive reinforcement to build trust and train your pet.
  • Provide mental stimulation with toys, puzzles, and interactive games.
  • Be patient as your pet adjusts to their new environment.
